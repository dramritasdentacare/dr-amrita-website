Know Your Dentist

Dr. Amrita Mishra (BDS,FCD)

Dr. Amrita Mishra is the visionary founder behind Dr. Amrita’s Dentacare. Built on a strong foundation of trust, comfort, and clinical excellence, the clinic reflects her dedication to patient first oral healthcare.

Dr. Amrita has over 5+ years of experience in creating healthy smiles, with a special focus on performing painless single-visit root canal treatments and cosmetic smile makeovers. She believes that a visit to the dentist should be completely comfortable and stress-free, and every treatment plan is carefully tailored to ensure long-term dental wellness.
